After leaving Cranston East, I went to Providence College (rooming with classmate Arnie Kirchenbaum for a year). I was accepted to the Brown University Medical School. I specialized in Ophthalmology (eye surgery) at Tufts in Boston and have been practicing in Milford and Wellesley, Massachusetts since 1984.

I have been happily married since 1974. My wife is Sr. Vice President & Chief Compliance Officer at Liberty Mutual Insurance Company in Boston. My daughter is graduated from Boston College and my son is attending Brown University.

Computer technology is a hobby of mine. The reason I wanted to put the website together is to interest others in staying in touch with former classmates and to get people on board with upcoming reunions. (I hope it works!).

I had a bit of a setback this year. I was diagnosed with cancer of the appendix in November, 2006. Some pretty grueling surgery was needed along with some chemotherapy. I've lost a lot of weight and have been out of work for several months. Things are looking up now however and I expect to be back to full steam by the Fall. The cards and good wishes from all have been a tremendous source of strength and support during this time.
