As many of you know who were with me as a young tyke at Hugh B. Bain Jr. HS, I always had a knack for raising money, selling tickets, etc. I continued to do this throughout college and actually funded a trip to Europe in 1973 with a weekly lottery!!

Little did I know that there was actually a career in fundraising. Since 1976, I have helped colleges, hospitals, churches, dioceses and many small non-profits raise money to help the poor, feed the hungry, build buildings and create endowments to provide scholarships for needy students. It sounds very noble, but actually, it has kept me low-key and its been a lot of fun.

Most important is that I am still with the girl I met at Scarborough beach the summer after Junior year and we have 2 great kids--Meghan and Conor.

I'm planning to be at the reunion and I look forward to seeing many longtime friends.
