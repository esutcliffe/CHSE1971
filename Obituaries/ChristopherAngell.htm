Christopher Bryant Angell,62, of Edgewood, passed away January 31, 2016 at R.I. Hospital, after a brief illness.

Born in Westfield, MA, son of David L. Angell and the late Ines A. {Filios} Angell. Chris had an A.S. degree in Marketing with a concentration in Purchasing.   He was an avid music fan and an associate of various local musicians. Chris was full of adventure, enjoyed the outdoors and was an avid surfer, skier, golfer and cyclist.

Besides his father he leaves a sister Alisa Kozlowski, her husband Henry and a brother David R. Angell, 3 nephews and 2 nieces. He was brother of the late Alan and Dan Angell.
