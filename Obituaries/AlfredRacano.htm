Alfredo M. Racano, 35, of Calaman Road, a self-employed exercise therapist in New York City for 15 years until illness, died yesterday at home.

Born in Providence, he was a son of Alfred M. and Anna (DelPonte) Racano of Cranston.

Besides his parents he leaves a brother, Michael E. Racano of Cranston, and his paternal grandmother, Mary (Bocci) Racano of Providence.
