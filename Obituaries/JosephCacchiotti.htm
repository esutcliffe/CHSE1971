CACCHIOTTI, JOSEPH (ACE)
63, former Director of Archives for NFL Films for 20 years, author of "Gridiron Gumshoe: My Life In and Out of the Vault" passed away April 14, 2017. He was born in Providence, Rhode Island and was the beloved son of Caroline (Alianiello) Cacchiotti and the late Joseph "Joe Cash" Cacchiotti. He was also the brother of the late Paula Cacchiotti-Mangili. He is survived by his mother Caroline, his wife Susan (Majeski) Cacchiotti, son Joe D. Cacchiotti, his sister Teresa Houle-Brierley, and nieces Rachel Pintarelli and Christina Houle. Private Burial.
