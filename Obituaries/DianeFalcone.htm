Diane M. (Falcone) Linden, 62, of Warwick, passed away peacefully on Sunday, November 29, 2015 at Rhode Island Hospital Providence. She was the beloved wife of twenty-five years to William J. Linden, Jr. Born in Providence, she was a daughter of the late Angelo and Celeste (Corsetti) Falcone. Diane was a billing coordinator for the law firm of Hinckley Allen, LLP in Providence for thirty years before her retirement.

Diane loved the classics. Walking into her home on any given day you would hear Frank Sinatra or Tony Bennet playing. She loved classic movies and would watch The Godfather or Gone with the Wind on many occasions. She loved shopping and always had something special and unique for her loved ones. Her favorite past time was spending time with her family, including her nieces and nephews and her greatest joy came from her two grandchildren, Samantha & Daniel.

Besides her husband, and his loving family, she is survived by her loving daughter, Kelli A. Giorno and her husband Natale of Cranston. She was the sister of Thomas Falcone of Warwick, Angela Falcone of Cranston, Carol Santagata of Boca Raton, FL, Patricia Lanigan and her husband Sean, and Maria Falcone, all of Cranston.
