Marc E. Plante, 59, of Gateway Court, passed away Thursday, January 17, 2013 at Kent Hospital. He was the beloved husband of Mary P. (Waldron) Plante and a son of Dolores (Lizotte) Plante and the late Frederick Plante. Marc was employed as a warehouse manager for the Choquette Co. for 25 years and later as a driver for the Airport Valet. He was a coach for the Apponaug Softball League for 20 years. Marc loved spending time with his family, going to the beach, and was a sports enthusiast. Besides his wife and mother, he is survived by three children, Alicia, Marc and Jessica Plante; two brothers Msg. Jacques Plante and Frederick Plante; one sister Monique Rameaka; one granddaughter Avery Plante.
