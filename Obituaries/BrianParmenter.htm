BRIAN W. PARMENTER, 47, of Washington Avenue, North White Plains, N.Y., a former employee of United Parcel Service, died Monday in Westchester Medical Center, Valhalla, N.Y.
Born in Providence, a son of Ruth W. (Wall) Hopkins of Cranston and the late William E. Parmenter Jr., and the stepson of Robert A. Hopkins of Cranston, he lived in New York for five years, and had previously lived in Connecticut for two years.
He was an operational manager for U.P.S. for 24 years, before retiring six years ago.
Mr. Parmenter was a graduate of the University of Rhode Island, Class of 1975.
Besides his mother and stepfather, he is survived by a daughter, a stepdaughter and a brother.
