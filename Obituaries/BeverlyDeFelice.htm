Beverly A. DeFelice, 62, of Wright Way, Coventry, RI, passed away on January 8, 2016 at Kent Hospital in Warwick, RI. She was the daughter of the late Lucien P. and Mary C. (Patnaude) Brisson. She was married to Richard L. DeFelice for twenty four years. Besides her husband, she leaves behind a step daughter Rhonda Moore and her husband Nathan and two grandsons, Allen and Quintin Moore. She is also survived by a twin sister Barbara (Brisson) Hardy and her husband Richard of Conway, South Carolina and a brother Paul Brisson and his wife Elaine of Merrimack, New Hampshire, as well as several nieces and nephews. She was employed by DELL until her retirement in 2014.
