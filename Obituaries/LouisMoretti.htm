Louis D. Moretti, 40, of East Avenue, a firefighter in the Cranston Fire Department for 17 years before retiring in 1990, died Sunday at home.
He was the husband of Sharon (Benjamin) Moretti of Warwick.
Born in Providence, a son of Mary (Ciorlano) Moretti of Cranston, and the late Louis D. Moretti Sr., he lived in Cranston most of his life.
Mr. Moretti was a member of the Cranston Firefighters Association.
Besides his wife and mother he is survived by a son, a sister and two brothers.
