Jacquelyn A. Lease, age 64, of Wellington, Florida, formally a resident of Rhode Island died unexpectantly of heart failure on January 7, 2018. She was the widow of the late John Grasso and the daughter of the late John W. and Alyce P. (Noel) Lease. She was survived by a number of cousins and friends in both Rhode Island and Florida who will miss her very much. Burial arrangements are private. Donations in her name can be made to any local animal shelter.
